About me

Daniel Caesar is a singer-songwriter from the United States. He was born on March 26, 1994, in New York City, New York, United States. He is known for his songs "Best Part", "Get You", and "Wait for U".

He has won many awards, including a Grammy Award for Best Pop Vocal Album. He is also known for his charity work, and he has donated to many causes, including the Red Cross and the American Red Cross.
